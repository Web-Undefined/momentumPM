Momentum is a third-party project management company acts as a neutral, professional coordinator that represents the owner’s best interests throughout a renovation or construction project. Instead of the owner managing everything themselves, the third-party PM company steps in to plan, organize, and oversee the entire process.

What we do

Planning & Pre-Construction

  • Define scope, schedule, and budget
  • Assist with design coordination, permits, and approvals
  • Help select architects, engineers, or contractors (or manage bidding)

Vendor Coordination

  • Source, vet, and coordinate all contractors, subcontractors, and suppliers
  • Serve as the single point of contact for everyone involved
  • Handle scheduling to avoid delays or conflicts

Project Oversight

  • Monitor progress and quality of work
  • Ensure the project stays on time and on budget
  • Address issues and change orders quickly

Communication & Reporting

  • Provide regular updates to the owner
  • Facilitate meetings and ensure clear communication between all parties
  • Document milestones, costs, and timelines

Post-Construction Closeout

  • Final walk-through and punch list management
  • Ensure permits are closed and inspections passed
  • Organize turnover of warranties, manuals, and final documents
